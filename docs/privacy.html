Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

InDays is a workplace presence tracker that helps hybrid workers log in-office days. This policy explains how the app handles your data — we keep things simple and transparent.

Data We Collect

InDays only stores the information you choose to enter while using the app. We do not collect information in the background or through outside tracking.

What Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored on your device and only moves to another device if you choose to sync:

  • Logs (dates and optional office location)
  • Office locations you create
  • History filters and target settings
  • Reminder preferences (time, active days, enabled/disabled)
  • Apple Watch companion data (today's status, office name, week count)

Optional Syncing

If you sign in, your data syncs securely so you can access it across devices. Syncing is entirely optional — the app works without an account.

Synced data includes the same categories listed above plus your sign-in details.

Signing In

InDays offers optional sign-in so you can sync your data across devices. We never see or store your password.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not include analytics, advertising, or any form of user tracking.

Reminders

InDays can send reminders to help you log your office day. These are scheduled entirely on your device — no data is sent anywhere. You can enable, disable, or adjust reminders at any time in the app.

Reminder notifications include a “Log today” action that records your visit directly without opening the app. This action uses your primary office location if one is set.

Apple Watch

The optional companion Watch app syncs a snapshot of your logged status, primary office name, and week count between your iPhone and Apple Watch. This data stays on your devices.

Logging a day from the Watch sends the entry back to your phone, where it is stored and can be synced like any other log.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

How Your Data Is Protected

Your synced data is protected so that each user can access only their own information. All data sent between devices is encrypted in transit.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual log entries from within the app. To delete your account and all synced data, email webdevsaad@gmail.com and we will process your request promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
