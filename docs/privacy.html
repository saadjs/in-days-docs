Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

InDays is a workplace presence tracker that helps hybrid workers log in-office days. This policy explains how the app handles your data — we keep things simple and transparent.

Data We Collect

InDays only stores the information you explicitly provide while using the app. We do not collect data passively in the background or through third-party tracking.

Local Storage

The following data is stored on your device and never leaves your phone unless you opt in to cloud sync:

  • Workday log entries (dates and optional office location)
  • Office locations you create
  • History filters and target settings
  • Reminder preferences (time, active days, enabled/disabled)
  • Apple Watch companion data (today's status, office name, week count)

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you sign in, your data syncs securely so you can access it across devices. Cloud sync is entirely optional — the app works fully offline without an account.

Synced data includes the same categories listed above plus your authentication profile (email address and display name from your sign-in provider).

Authentication

InDays offers optional sign-in so you can sync your data across devices. We never see or store your password.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not include analytics, advertising, or any form of user tracking.

Notifications

InDays can send local notifications to remind you to log your office day. These are scheduled entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server. You can enable, disable, or configure reminders at any time in the app.

Reminder notifications include a “Log today” action that records your visit directly without opening the app. This action runs entirely on-device using your primary office location if one is set.

Apple Watch

The optional companion Watch app syncs a lightweight snapshot (logged status, primary office name, and week count) between your iPhone and Apple Watch using WatchConnectivity. This data is stored locally on both devices — no server is involved.

Logging a day from the Watch syncs the entry back to your phone, where it is stored and optionally synced to the cloud like any other log entry.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Data Security

Cloud data is protected so that each user can access only their own data. All network traffic is encrypted in transit.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual log entries from within the app. To delete your account and all associated cloud data, email webdevsaad@gmail.com and we will process your request promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
